Grilled PBJ Sandwich

Description

The simple yet so delicious peanut butter and jelly sandwhich can be a perfect breakfast on the go or just the right snack.

Ingredients

  • 2 teaspoons butter
  • 2 slices white bread
  • 2 teaspoons strawberry jelly
  • 1 teaspoon peanut butter

Steps

  1. Gather ingredients
  2. Heat griddle to 350 degrees Farenheit
  3. Spread butter on one side of each slice of bread. Spread jelly on the unbuttered side of one slice of bread; spread peanut butter on the other unbuttered side
  4. Place bread slices together, so peanut butter and jelly are in the middle
  5. Cook on griddle until golden brown about 4 mins per side
