Buttermilk Pancakes

Description

As convenient as store bought batter can be, nothing truly beats homemade pancakes, which is why this recipe will teach you everything you need to know.

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • 3 tablespoons Sugar
  • Baking Powder
  • Baking Soda
  • Pinch of Salt
  • 3 cups Buttermilk
  • 1 cup whole Milk
  • 3 Eggs
  • 1/4 stick Butter

Steps

  1. Mix the dry Ingredients: Combine flour, sugar, baking powder, baking soda, and salt into a bowl
  2. Mix the wet Ingredients: Beat the Buttermilk, milk, eggs, and melted butter together into a seperate bowl.
  3. Make the pancakes: Add the wet ingredients to the dry ingredients and mix with a wooden spoon until blended. Scoop the batter onto a hot, oiled griddle and cook until the pancake is bubble on the top.
  4. Cook until golden: After a few mins flip with a spatual and cook until both sides are golden brown
