Scrambled Eggs

Description

This recipe focuses on making soft and creamy eggs, which can only be done a certain way: nonstickpan, low heat, and patience!

Ingredients

  • 1 tablespoon butter
  • 4 large eggs,beater
  • 1/4 cup cottage cheese
  • 1 teaspoon chopped fresh chives
  • ground black pepper

Steps

  1. Gather ingredients
  2. Melt butter in a skiller over medium heat. Pour beaten eggs into the skillet; let cook undisturbed until bottom of the eggs begins to firm, 1-2 mins
  3. Stir cottage cheese and chives into eggs and season with black pepper
  4. Cook and stir until eggs are nearly set, 3-4 mins more
